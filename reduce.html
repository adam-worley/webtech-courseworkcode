WHAT WE ARE DOING

At TRITEC we focus on three key areas to reduce our environmental impact. The first area to focus on is our energy consumption and carbon emmisions, to reduce this we have utilised numerous energy saving methods and even installed our own renewable energy system. Our second focus is a reduction in waste, in particular a reduction of non-recyclable waste and single use plastics. Our final focus is a reduction in our cunsumption of water Although water is not a big consumable for us or a scarce resource in the UK we believe that part of being a sustainable business is using as little of the Earth's resources as possible. Please see below to find out exactly what we are doing in each area.

ENERGY


  • We have installed Photovoltaic solar panels to all of our UK facilities to produce our own electricity emission free. In the last 12 months we produced approximately 16% of all the electricity used, and during the 3 year period they have been installed we have saved the equivantly carbon emissions of planting 130 trees

  • All of our UK facilities feature the latest A+ rated LED lighting systems which reduces our carbon emmissions by approximately 12Kg per year over the old system.(The same as a medium size tree absorbs each year)

  • Every kitchen in our facilities features a boiling water tap that on average saves 14% energy per cup.

WASTE


  • We no longer supply plastic cups by our water coolers to reduce our single plastics use and waste. Employees and visistors are instead encourage to use the glasses that can be found in each kitchen


  • We have recently gone paperless wherever possible to reduce our waste and the consumption of paper.


  • We reduce waste by reusing 100% of the shipping materials from goods inwards. (find out more about this on the reuse page)

WATER


  • Every tap within our facilities is fitted with a tap aerator which can save up 1200 litres per tap per month.


  • Every toilet within our facilities is fitted with a dual flush system which halves the water reduce toilet water usuage by 50%


  • Our facilities are fitted with waterless urinals that utilse naturally occuring microbes and enzymes to stay sanitary. Each waterless urinals save approzimately 20,000 liters of water per year over their flushing counterparts.






WHAT YOU CAN DO TO HELP

To be successful in our mission we need every member of the TRITEC family to get involved and do their bit. please read below to find out how you can help in this area.

ENERGY

  • Turn off office lights on a bright day, we have plenty of natural light through the windows and the natural light has been scientiffically proven to boost mood and improve productivity

  • Turn off all lights if you're the last person to leave a room

  • Ensure all monitors and PC's are switched off at the end of the day. Adjust the power settings on your PC/laptop to screen sleep after 5 minutes and sleep after 30 minutes to save power whilst you're away from your desk.

WASTE

  • Use tupperware containers if you bring a packed lunch to avoid using single use plastics (clingfilm or sandwhich bags)


  • Only print a document if absolutely neccessary



  • Keep all packing materials from any recieved goods to be reused.

WATER

  • Ensure you utilise the half flush where applicable



  • Ensure all taps are securely closed after use to avoid dripping taps. A leaking tap can waste over 125 litres of water per year



  • Don't leave a tap running unless you are using it directly
