WHAT WE ARE DOING

Whilst we would love to reduce our waste to 0 we appreciate that it this isn't feasible. Reuse goes hand in hand with reduce from a waste aspect as the more items we reuse the less waste we produce. At TRITEC we have two key areas we wish to focus our REUSE effort. The first is Reusing plastic where posible or swapping to a reusable substitute where single use plastics have been used previously. The second area is packing materials. The nature of our business means we have a large amount of incoming goods, excluding tape every single item of packing material (boxes & bubble wrap etc) and even pallets can be reused for storage in warehouse or when shipping goods to our customers.

PLASTIC


  • All our single use plastic cups have been replaced with reusable glasses, these can be found in the cupboards in each kitchen.


  • One of the only single use plastics we had in use was grip seal bags. We implemented a business wide policy that states that only damaged bags can be disposed of, all other empty bags can go into a seperate used area to be reused internally. This saves approximately 350 bags per week from being disposed.

PACKING MATERIALS


  • We have a 100% reuse policy on all recieved packageing (where posible). As long as boxes are unbranded they will be reused for outgoing orders, warehouse even have the ability to cut down boxes and reproduce multiple smaller boxes. For all packaging padding material we place it bins with similar supplies to be reused for customer orders.


  • We reuse 100% of on pallets and shipping crates, these are stored in warehouse ready for large orders and when applicable sent to customers






WHAT YOU CAN DO TO HELP

To be successful in our mission we need every member of the TRITEC family to get involved and do their bit. please read below to find out how you can help in this area.

PLASTIC

  • Utilise a reusable plastic bottle for drinks and avoid single use plastics if possible. Alternatively please help yourself to a glass for water (just make sure you wash it up)


  • Take care when opening and closing grip seal bags to increase their longevity. Remember to reuse grip seal bags and only discard them if they become damaged

PACKING MATERIALS

  • Take care when opening goods in packages to ensure the packing materials don't get damaged.

  • Make sure any recieved packing materials make their way to warehouse to reused.

  • Only recycle any packing material if it is damaged beyond reuse.
