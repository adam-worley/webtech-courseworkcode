OUR COMMITMENT

At TRITEC we are commited to making a change today for a better tomorrow. We believe the only thing we all have in common is our planet and therefor we all have a responsibility to ensure it is protected for generations to come. The TRITEC family are proud to announce our new commitment to improve the sustainabilty and environmental impact of our operations, we endeaver to improve all aspects of our business to reduce our carbon footprint, consumption and waste. Over the next 5 Years we have 3 distinct targets we are committed to achieve:

  1. Reduce energy consumption by a further 20%
  2. Reduce waste by 25%
  3. For 80% of our waste to be recylcable

This will be no easy task and to be succussful we can't just rely on a few people implementing big changes, we need every single member of the TRITEC family to implement small changes in their worklife. The foundation for our commitment is the reduce, reuse, recycle model and more information on each area can be found on the alternate pages. We are proud of everything we achieve as the TRITEC family and have no doubt that if we all work together this is another great target we can achieve.

Please use this as resource to find out what we are doing as business and what you can do to help.

We appreacite that everybody has a different dynamic of thought and you may have some ideas as to how we can improve as a green business. Please visit the suggestions page to share your ideas.
